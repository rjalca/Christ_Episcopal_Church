Site Intent

The redesign of Christ Episcopal Church's website will be focused on creating a modern, organized website catering to the needs and desires of the targeted audience. The plan includes having a structured navigation system to assist in the ease of use for the site's visitors. The redesigned website will also include a logo to represent the goals of the church.

Competitive Analysis

The three main churches found through a google search of churches in Seattle, Washington are Mars Mill Church, The City Church, and Seattle Baptist Church. According to research from Alexa (The Web Information Company), these three churches' main browsers are males from ages 25 to 34 years with a college education. Their websites are a complete contrast to that of Christ Episcopal Church's website which seems to target an older group of christians

Targeted Audience

Based on the information gathered from ZipSkinny's website, the target audience for this redesign will be single individuals ages 20 to 29 years. 69% of the individuals in the near vicinity of the church are well educated having achieved a minimum of a bachelor's degree. The majority of this target audience work as office professionals and in management.
